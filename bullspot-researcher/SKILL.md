# Bullspot Researcher Skill

## When to Use
- Researching competitors
- Market trend analysis
- Finding blog topics
- Competitive intelligence

## Competitors to Track
- TradingView, eToro, ZuluTrade, Coinigy, 3Commas, TradeSanta

## Tools
- blogwatcher - Monitor competitor blogs
- polymarket - Prediction market data
- arxiv - Academic crypto/trading research

## Research Schedule
- Weekly: Competitor monitoring
- Daily: Crypto news scan
- Before content: Full topic research

## Output Format
For competitor reports: Overview, pricing, features, content strategy, weaknesses, opportunities
