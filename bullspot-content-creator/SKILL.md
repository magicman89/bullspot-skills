# Bullspot Content Creator Skill

## When to Use
- Creating social media posts for Twitter, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube
- Writing Twitter threads
- Creating TikTok video scripts
- Writing Instagram captions and hashtags
- Creating YouTube video outlines and descriptions
- Generating images for posts

## Bullspot Core Selling Points (ALWAYS incorporate at least one)
1. **Fully Autonomous Agentic AI Trading 24/7** - "Set it and forget it" - AI never sleeps
2. **Serious Risk Management** - Multiple bilevel stops + proper risk/reward management built in
3. **Self-Improving** - Auto-learns from mistakes over time, gets smarter

## Content Types Per Platform

### Twitter/X (@Bull_Spot)
- Threads (5-8 tweets): Educational, market analysis, feature highlights
- Single tweets: Quick tips, chart commentary, engagement posts
- Polls: Engagement drivers, market sentiment
- Quote tweets: Engage with crypto influencers, add value

### Instagram (@bullspot.app)
- Carousels: Educational (5-10 slides), feature explanations
- Single posts: Quotes, stats, chart screenshots
- Reels: Dashboard demos, quick tips, trending audio
- Stories: Polls, Q&A, behind-the-scenes

### TikTok (@bullspot.app)
- POV videos: "POV: You're sleeping while your AI trades"
- Tutorial clips: 30-60 sec feature walkthroughs
- Meme format: Relatable crypto trader content
- Trend participation: Adapt trending sounds to Bullspot themes

### YouTube (@bullspot)
- Long-form: Dashboard tutorials, market analysis, expert interviews
- Shorts: Quick tips, feature demos, market recaps
- Thumbnails: Bold text, charts, expressive faces

## Image Generation Guidelines
- **Theme**: Dark background (#0a0e27), neon green (#00FF88) and cyan (#00F0FF) accents
- **Style**: Professional, data-dense, modern fintech aesthetic
- **Format**: 1080x1080 (Instagram), 1200x675 (Twitter), 1080x1920 (TikTok)
- **Tools**: Use stable-diffusion-image-generation or MiniMax

## Posting Schedule
- **Twitter**: 7AM, 12PM, 6PM EST (peak crypto hours)
- **Instagram**: 8AM, 1PM, 7PM EST
- **TikTok**: 6PM-9PM EST (highest engagement)
- **YouTube**: 10AM EST
- **Medium**: Sunday evenings
