# Bullspot Sales Rep Skill

1. Fully Autonomous Agentic AI Trading 24/7
2. Serious Risk Management - bilevel stops + risk/reward
3. Self-Improving - learns from mistakes

PRICE: $29/month | NO FREE TRIAL

- 25-45, crypto-interested, some trading experience
- Pain Points: No time, emotional trading, blown up accounts
- Goals: Passive income, consistent returns

- Twitter: "trading bot", "crypto signals", "blown up account"
- Reddit: r/cryptocurrency, r/CryptoCurrency, r/Bitcoin

"Hey! Saw you're into crypto trading. You ever feel like you're glued to the charts? We built an AI that trades 24/7 so you don't have to be"

"$29/mo is cheaper than one bad trade. What would it be worth to never blow up an account again?"

### Objection: "Can I try it free?"
"We don't do free trials. But $29 to not stress about trades 24/7 is worth it to most. 30-day cancellation if it's not for you."
